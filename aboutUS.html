Company Information

Welcome to PharmaTechSupply, your trusted partner in the healthcare industry. We are a leading provider of medical technology supplies, offering a wide range of products and solutions to meet the evolving needs of healthcare professionals and organizations. At PharmaTechSupply, we pride ourselves on our deep industry knowledge and expertise. With over 15 years of experience in the medical technology field, we understand the unique challenges and demands of the healthcare sector. Our team of dedicated professionals brings their extensive industry insights to ensure we deliver the highest quality products and services to our valued customers. Our commitment to excellence is reflected in our wide selection of medical technology supplies. We offer a comprehensive catalog of products, including diagnostic tools, medical equipment, surgical instruments, consumables, and more. Our vast selection allows healthcare providers to find the right solutions to support their clinical practices and enhance patient care. We stay at the forefront of the industry by embracing the latest advancements in medical technology. By partnering with leading manufacturers and suppliers, we offer innovative and state-of-the-art products that incorporate the latest technologies. We are dedicated to providing healthcare professionals with access to the most advanced tools and equipment to optimize their practice and improve patient outcomes. At PharmaTechSupply, we understand that exceptional service goes hand in hand with quality products. Our customer-centric approach ensures that we provide personalized support, timely deliveries, and responsive customer service. We strive to build long-term relationships with our customers based on trust, reliability, and mutual success. Partner with PharmaTechSupply and experience the difference of working with a trusted provider of medical technology supplies. We are committed to supporting your healthcare practice with our deep industry knowledge, wide selection of products, and dedication to delivering the latest technology solutions. Together, let's shape the future of healthcare.

Jack Tiwari, CEO

Jack Tiwari is a CEO/Co-Founder of PharmaTechSupply.com, a leading technology company in the healthcare industry. With over 15 years of experience in the real estate sector, Jack has established himself as a prominent figure in Northern Virginia. He brings a wealth of knowledge and expertise to his role, leveraging his extensive network and deep understanding of the local market. In addition to his accomplishments in real estate, Jack has also co-founded two successful ventures. He was a co-founder of Foodmerica.com, an innovative platform connecting food enthusiasts with unique culinary experiences. Jack's passion for entrepreneurship led him to co-found ArvinFashion, a fashion e-commerce company known for its curated collection of trendy and sustainable clothing. With his diverse background and entrepreneurial spirit, Jack continues to drive innovation and make a positive impact in the business world. He is dedicated to creating transformative solutions and empowering others to achieve their goals.

Within 30 days of your original ship date, you can send in your product(s) with our paid postage return slip included in origina packaging and recieve a full refund.

Yes, we understand the importance of evaluating products before committing to a large order. In some cases, we may be able to provide samples of healthcare products upon request. Please contact our customer support team to discuss your sample request and availability.

Depending on the type of healthcare supplies and your location, there may be specific requirements or regulations. We encourage you to consult with your local regulatory authorities or reach out to our customer support for guidance on any specific requirements.

Shipping & Returns

We want you to be happy. If you are dissatisfied with your purchase, we would love to make it right, either by fixing what bothers you, exchanging the product for a different one, or giving a full refund (including original shipping). If you would like to make a return, send the item back to us in its original condition with all documentation and packaging within 30 days of the date you received the item. Please package the item carefully and insure your shipment for the appropriate value. We cannot take responsibility for lost, damaged, or stolen items. After we receive and verify the shipment, please allow 7-10 days for the full refund to be credited back to you via the payment method you used to purchase the item. We reserve the right to refuse any exchange or refund if we believe this policy is being abused.
