Executive Team

CEO

Jack Tiwari is a co-founder of PharmaTechSupply.com, a leading technology company in the healthcare industry. With over 15 years of experience in the real estate sector, Jack has established himself as a prominent figure in Northern Virginia. He brings a wealth of knowledge and expertise to his role, leveraging his extensive network and deep understanding of the local market. In addition to his accomplishments in real estate, Jack has also co-founded two successful ventures. He was a co-founder of Foodmerica.com, an innovative platform connecting food enthusiasts with unique culinary experiences. Jack's passion for entrepreneurship led him to co-found ArvinFashion, a fashion e-commerce company known for its curated collection of trendy and sustainable clothing. With his diverse background and entrepreneurial spirit, Jack continues to drive innovation and make a positive impact in the business world. He is dedicated to creating transformative solutions and empowering others to achieve their goals.
